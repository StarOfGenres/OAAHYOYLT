Life Simulation

How long will you live? How tough could life be?

Life is just too short. Once you die you will be gone from this universe, and I mean that. What really matters is now. You’re still alive. You are still able to make changes.

This Life Simulation game is a metaphor for life.

You will walk towards the problem. You will be born and suffer through life and finally, you will face death. Once you had a dream. Once you had a goal. Once you have made mistakes. But Eventually, you move forward. You might have been successful and you may still fail. You may fail, but you could still be successful. It’s your choice here.

There are 2 versions available: Desktop and Mobile.

It is highly recommended to play the PC version instead as the mobile version doesn’t have sound and the resolution is broken.
